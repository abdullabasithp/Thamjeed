About Us

Welcome to Thamjeed

Welcome to Thamjeed, a pioneering online platform dedicated to making Islamic,Quran education and Arabic language learning accessible and personalized for all. Our mission is to empower individuals, especially children, to confidently recite the Quran with proper Tajweed ,learn Islamic education and Arabic verbal skills.

Thamjeed offers individual classes for each student. Through our one-to-one tutoring method, we will make your child’s future better and brighter. One to one tuition is the best way to get personalized attention. Choose the subject you need and attend classes at your preferred time. Join us on this enriching journey, and let's unlock the beauty of the Quran and Arabic language together.
